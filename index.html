Providing hours of fun and education

Play Store Fun is a realistic checkout system that fosters the imagination while learning is taking place.

Get Play Store Fun

Use any item with a barcode.

You get a barcode scanner that can scan any barcode. This means that any item with an existing barcode may be used with the Play Store Fun system. We will provide you with barcodes that can be added to items that don't already have barcodes.

Setup many different types of stores.

The Play Store Fun checkout system can be used to create grocery stores, coffee shops, libraries, movie rental stores, and much much more.

Learn basic math skills.

You will get play money that you can use to pay for "purchases", and make change.
