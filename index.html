"Let their imaginations run wild!"

Play Store Fun is a realistic checkout system that fosters imaginative play while providing hours of fun AND education!

Get Play Store Fun

*Chromebook not included. User must provide an internet connected computer with USB port and web camera.

Set up many different types of stores!

The Play Store Fun checkout system can be used to create a variety of stores such as grocery stores, pet stores, coffee shops, libraries and more! From experience with our own children, much of the fun comes with creating and setting up all new shops!

Use any existing barcodes!

The included barcode scanner can be used to scan any item with a barcode, not just the barcode stickers included. That means you can use barcodes on books, food items, toy packaging, board games, and more!

Teaches basic money math!

The Play Store Fun starter kit includes realistic play money that can be used to “pay” for purchases and make change. It can help young children learn basic money math skills. They’ll be having so much fun playing, they won’t even realize they’re learning!

Make memories that will last a lifetime!

In an age where unlimited screen time has become the norm, why not invest in the opportunity to give your child the gift of imaginative play? Whether they’re playing alone, with siblings, with friends or with you, kids are sure to make unforgettable memories with Play Store Fun!

One of my favorite pastimes as a child was spending hours setting up stores and then pretending to have customers. It’s been so fun watching my kids be able to experience that same type of imaginative play and much more thanks to Play Store Fun!
